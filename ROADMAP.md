# Light Matters Roadmap

This roadmap is intended to track the next meaningful feature steps for `Light Matters` as the prototype becomes a more complete survival mod.

## Current Focus

- Refine the compact darkness HUD after live playtesting.
- Balance `Calming Tea` and `Miner's Tonic` around actual expedition pacing.
- Continue tuning outdoor darkness across weather, dusk, night, and dawn.
- Continue polishing lantern presentation within the survival progression only.

## Near-Term Features

- Add safe-zone recovery around campfires, beds, and strong light sources.
- Add more fuel types with stronger tradeoffs between efficiency, rarity, and burn time.
- Improve `Panic` with stronger aim sway and combat pressure.
- Improve `Fatigue` with more interaction penalties and slower task handling.
- Add clearer audio feedback for lantern refueling, extinguishing, and tier identity.

## Mid-Term Features

- Add dimension-specific darkness rules for the Overworld, Nether, and End.
- Add biome and weather-specific darkness modifiers.
- Add stronger mob or world interactions based on light quality.
- Expand world lighting with more permanent base-light options such as braziers, lamps, or camp lanterns.
- Add more advanced progression hooks for prepared players pushing into deeper darkness.

## Longer-Term Polish

- Expand configuration and accessibility options.
- Add better onboarding through advancements, hints, or guide text.
- Continue visual and audio differentiation between lantern tiers.
- Build out testing and balancing commands for faster iteration.
